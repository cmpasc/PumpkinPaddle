Welcome

Western Mass. man paddles the Connecticut River in a pumpkin ...Gary Kristensen holds the Guinness World Record for the longest journey by pumpkin boat, paddling a 1,200+ pound pumpkin 58.57 miles (94.26 km) down the Columbia River in Oregon on May 24, 2025. He broke his own 2024 record (45.67 miles) using a, "Marchflower" pumpkin, often paddling in strong spring currents.
